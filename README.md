Data Storytelling & Statistical Validation (Task 4)
Project Overview

This project focuses on data storytelling and statistical validation using a sales dataset. The objective of this task is to transform analytical insights into a clear business narrative and validate key findings using statistical methods.

The analysis combines Python-based data analysis, Power BI dashboard insights, and hypothesis testing to demonstrate how data-driven decisions can support business strategy.

Objective

The main objective of this project is to analyze sales data to understand:

Sales performance across different product categories

Regional sales distribution

Customer behavior patterns

Business insights that can improve decision-making

The project also applies statistical hypothesis testing to validate whether product category significantly affects sales amount.

The dataset contains sales transaction information including product details, customer information, and revenue data.

Data Analysis Process

The project was completed through the following steps:

1 Data Cleaning

The dataset was inspected for missing values and formatted to ensure accurate analysis.

2 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Python was used to explore the dataset and identify patterns, trends, and relationships between variables.

3 Data Visualization

Charts and graphs were created to better understand sales trends and category performance.

4 Dashboard Creation

A Power BI dashboard was developed to visualize key metrics such as:

Sales by Product Category

Monthly Sales Trends

Revenue by Region

Top Customers

Sales Channel Performance

Key Insights

The analysis revealed several important insights:

Certain product categories generate significantly higher revenue than others.

Sales performance varies across different regions.

Some months experience higher sales compared to others.

Returning customers contribute significantly to total revenue.

Discounts and payment methods also influence purchasing behavior.

These insights can help businesses focus on high-performing areas and optimize their sales strategies.

Hypothesis Testing

To statistically validate the findings, a hypothesis test was performed.

Business Question

Does product category significantly affect sales amount?

Null Hypothesis (H₀)

Product category does not significantly affect sales amount.

Alternative Hypothesis (H₁)

Product category significantly affects sales amount.

Statistical Test Used

ANOVA (Analysis of Variance)

Result

The statistical test produced a p-value less than 0.05, indicating that product category has a statistically significant impact on sales amount.

Conclusion

The null hypothesis was rejected, confirming that product category influences revenue generation.

Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are suggested:

Focus marketing efforts on high-performing product categories.

Expand sales strategies in regions with strong revenue performance.

Encourage repeat purchases by offering loyalty programs for returning customers.

Optimize pricing and discount strategies to maximize revenue.
